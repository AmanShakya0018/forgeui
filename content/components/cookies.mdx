It provides a user-friendly interface that prompts visitors to accept or customize their cookie preferences. The component is customizable, allowing for various styles and configurations to match your website's design. It is responsive and can be easily integrated into any React application, offering a seamless way to handle cookie-related consent and improve user trust and experience.
