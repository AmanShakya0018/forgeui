The Alerts component enables developers to easily display contextual notifications, such as success, error, or warning messages, to users. Styled using Tailwind CSS, it offers a clean and responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes. The component is highly customizable, allowing developers to adjust colors, iconography, and positioning to match their application's needs. This component is ideal for informing users about system events, form submissions, or critical updates, enhancing overall user experience with clear and concise messaging.
