The Footer component in React provides a structured section at the bottom of web pages, offering users quick access to valuable information. It is ideal for displaying sitemap links, copyright notices, contact details, or social media links. Customizable in layout, style, and content, the Footer enhances the website's navigation and professionalism. Designed for responsiveness, it adapts seamlessly to various screen sizes, ensuring accessibility on all devices. This component enriches user experience by consolidating critical details in an intuitive and easily discoverable location.
