<div className="mdx-body">
The TextArea component is designed to display a form textarea, allowing users to input and edit multiline text. It is fully customizable in terms of size, styling, and behavior to fit a variety of form requirements. This component can be integrated into applications requiring input fields such as comment sections, user messages, or any context where longer text is needed. It supports responsiveness to ensure a seamless user experience across different devices and screen sizes.

```js showLineNumbers caption="You can customize the markup and modify the tags as per your preferred framework to seamlessly integrate them into your project."
<div>
  <label htmlFor="message" className="block mb-2 text-sm font-medium text-gray-900 dark:text-white">
    Your message
  </label>
  <textarea
    id="message"
    rows={4}
    className="block p-2.5 w-full text-sm text-gray-900 bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-300 dark:bg-black dark:border-gray-600 dark:text-white dark:placeholder-gray-400"
    placeholder="Write your thoughts here..."
  ></textarea>
</div>
```
